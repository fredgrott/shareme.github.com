Slide #12

Do not forget the Theme that sets the Title width as since we are using the Window Extended features we have to define a title width, otherwise we will get a default title width...first the style grabbing the default window background..

1 <?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
2     <resources>
3       <style name="WindowTitleBackground"
4           parent="android:WindowTitleBackground">
5            <item name="android:background">
6                @android:color/transparent</item>
7       </style>
8     </resources>

Than our theme which we define in the sometimes in the application tag

 1 <?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
 2    <resources>
 3      <style name="MyTheme" parent="android:Theme">
 4         <item name="android:windowTitleSize">54px</item>
 5         <item name="android:windowTitleBackgroundStyle">
 6             @style/WindowTitleBackground</item>
 7         <item name="android:windowContentOverlay">
 8             @drawable/title_bar_shadow2</item>
 9      </style>
10   </resources>

Built for and by nerds

Fred Grott is an Android geek who loves to put well designed and developed android applications in users hands. At an early age he started drawing and programming and both areas always cary over into the user experience I convey in my applications.

He is known for pushing the envelop, he was creating applications using the full homescreen as the user interface in an application before such techniques spread with introduction of android 3.0.

Agile is an Artist Process

Most developers do not like agile processes. But, ask any artist how many fast iterations they do to reveiw with their innner skeptic and you alwqys hear a lot.

The agile process is about preparing and embracing change. Fred Grott strives to discover the next set of development process before its becolmes vogue.

Fragmentatin Proofed

Fred Grott has developed an android development process that fragmentation proofs and future proofs an android application both in the user-interface and the underlying code components thus minimizing the frequency of updates to an application you would ask for as the android OS matures.

Non-Google Experience Devices

Fred Grott specializes also in user-interface design and development of applications for non-Google Experience devices such as OPhone, KinldeFire, Nook, etc.

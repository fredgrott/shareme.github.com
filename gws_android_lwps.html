GWS Moar Snow

An Android LiveWallpaper displaying blowing snow for that peaceful snowed-in-feeling. The apk can be downloaded from the bitbucket repo.

GWS LogWallpaper

An Android LiveWallpaper for those who are curious about all those logcat messages. It does use permissions to read logs, thus if that bothers you than choose not to use it or not to download it. It can be downloaded from the bitbucket repo.
